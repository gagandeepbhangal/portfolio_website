    Bhangra

    Bhangra is a cultural Punjabi dance form. Much like how Hip Hop and Ballet area dance forms, so is Bhangra. I have been learning Bhangra since I was 14 years old and now I am a professional dancer on the team Nachdi Jawani Joshiley. In the video to the right, you can see me doing what I do best in the colour Royal blue! I have performed at hundreds of weddings, anniversaries, competitions and multicultural events. My most notable achievement is winning 1st place at the Circle City Bhangra competition in Indianapolis, US. Along with my 15 teammates, we trained tirelessly to put together one of the best performances in Nachdi Jawani's (my academy) history! Bhangra is something I will always love and one day pass on to my children.

    Soccer

    Soccer is my most loved and oldest sport in my life. I have been playing soccer since I was only 5 years old! I have been part of the Brampton Youth Soccer Club for 13 years. I participated in House League where I have won the league title numerous times over the years. My most satisfying victory came in the 2013 league final where we (the underdogs) beat the undefeated Royal Blue team where I scored the winning penalty! The trophy for this is the large one in the middle of the picture on the right. My favourite player is Lionel Messi whom I consider the best player of all time. I aspire to be as technically gifted as he is both on and off the pitch. Although Messi is a forward, I primarily played full back as it was what I primarily exceeded at. Whenever I have nothing to do and want to get some exercise in, you can always find me on the nearest soccer pitch!

    Badminton

    Badminton is a sport that made up my entire High School years. From grade 9, I picked up a Badminton raquet and turned out to actually be pretty good at the sport. I think I get it from my mom as she was extremely good at Badminton from a young age. From there, I met a friend who complemented my play style perfectly. I am more of an aggressive player with power whereas he was a more delicate player with precision. Together, we went on to dominate the Brampton Badminton scene for all 4 years. Every year, me and my partner would win MVP due to our success (as seen in the picture to the right). In grade 12, we even made it to the provincial finals! Thus, Badminton will always hold a special place in my heart and will be a sport I play and teach my children in the future.
